2020 is set to be an exciting year for DeFi. Lending markets are more competitive than ever. Countless layer 2 solutions are being built with new ZK technology. And to top it off, this year we’re likely to see interoperability make great strides. Let’s dive into a few DApps who have hit the ground running this year.<\/p>\n

Maker<\/h3>\n

Fresh off the successful launch of Multi-Collateral Dai, Maker<\/a> has been solidifying its #1 position on the DeFi Pulse<\/a> rankings headed into 2020. As of writing this, there’s over 36M DAI locked<\/a> in MakerDAO, primarily in the Dai Savings Rate. And now, MakerDAO voted to increase the Dai Savings Rate<\/a> (DSR<\/a>) to 6%. It’s no wonder the total DAI locked in DeFi is setting a new all-time high.<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

To add fuel to the fire, CHAI<\/a>\u00a0is a smart contract that wraps DAI in the DSR so DeFi users can have their cake and eat it too. Chai can also be used in smart contracts. For example, Fulcrum’s iDAI now supports CHAI so any idle DAI earns the DSR.<\/p>\n
