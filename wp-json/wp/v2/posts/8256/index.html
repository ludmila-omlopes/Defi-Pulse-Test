Liquidity is vital for the growth of DeFi<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Users want to experience all DeFi has to offer. But often times to do that you need to swap between tokens or stablecoins. Liquidity is an important resource that enables DeFi’s composability and makes it possible for many DApps to function. It’s the blood that flows through the veins of DeFi. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

For a smooth user experience, it’s essential to have a reliable liquidity at a decent price. DEXes are an obvious choice for reliable liquidity when designing an application or smart contract. However, relying on a single DEX comes with its disadvantages.
