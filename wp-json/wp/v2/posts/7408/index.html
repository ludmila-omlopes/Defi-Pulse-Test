Thanks to the support of the community DeFi Pulse has quickly become a resource people turn to when discussing decentralized finance. Up until now, DeFi Pulse only ranks on-chain DeFi projects. But we know that the DeFi community is larger than what\u2019s listed on the leaderboard. And some aspects of our community aren\u2019t as easily measured as a smart contract. So that\u2019s why we\u2019re adding to DeFi Pulse what we call The DeFi List<\/a>.<\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0
\n<\/span><\/p>\n

The DeFi List is a collection of the best DeFi resources, and a new way for all the DeFi community to grow together. Anyone helping build DeFi has an opportunity to be on the list.\u00a0<\/span>Whether you\u2019re a project, newsletter, news site, blog, analytics site, or service provider, a<\/span>nyone can apply. Check out the application form<\/a> for specific details.<\/p>\n

We’ll take the best applications and add them to our list helping our users discover your work. If you’re included, we’ll add a short description and a link to your site to the list. And in order to be listed on the DeFi Pulse Leaderboard, you must be included on this list.<\/span><\/p>\n

Learning about decentralized finance just got a little easier<\/h3>\n

Our hope is that this list will provide a great resource for newcomers and veterans alike. Aiding their research and helping them to discover DeFi projects. Being included in The DeFi List signals to users that the site they are visiting has been perceived as being of high value within the DeFi community. Also, it helps new users understand what it is and is not decentralized finance.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

We want to use DeFi Pulse to help highlight where the community is growing. In addition to be included on The DeFi List, you\u2019ll have the opportunity to post marketing announcements to the DeFi Pulse discord channel. And have your announcements broadcasted to the broader DeFi community via the\u00a0@DefiPulse Twitter<\/a> (at social media manager\u2019s discretion). The DeFi community grows bigger and gets stronger collectively with every new addition. And we want to world to know it!<\/span><\/p>\n
