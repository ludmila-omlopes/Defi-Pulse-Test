The DeFi Pulse Index is a digital asset index designed to track tokens\u2019 performance within the Decentralized Finance industry. The index is weighted based on the value of each token\u2019s circulating supply. As part of our <\/strong>commitment to the ongoing maintenance and development of the DeFi Pulse Index, we are transitioning from v0.1 of our methodology to <\/strong>DeFi Pulse Index Construction, Version 0.2<\/strong><\/a>. <\/strong>Version 0.2 introduces a change to the inclusion criteria that tokens must have a circulating supply minimum that is 7.5% or higher to ensure tokens have sufficient liquidity.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

As outlined in v0.2 of its methodology, the DeFi Pulse Index is maintained in two phases: the Determination Phase and the Reconstitution Phase. The Determination Phase takes place during the third week of the month. During this phase, the circulating supply of each token currently included in DPI is determined. Any tokens eligible for addition or removal from DPI are also determined during this phase. Finally, new index weights, additions and deletions are incorporated into the index during the monthly Reconstitution Phase on the first business day of the month. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
