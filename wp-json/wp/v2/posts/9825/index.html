DeFi is a fast moving and highly competitive space. While DeFi may level the playing field by allowing anyone to participate, it still comes with risks and has a steep learning curve. When these factors are combined, it\u2019s easy to see how the average user may feel left behind at times. Built by the team at Bloq, Vesper Finance<\/a> attempts to lower the barrier to entry for users by striking a balance between professionalism and DeFi\u2019s dynamism in its products. Vesper\u2019s first product \u2014 Vesper Grow, originally introduced as \u201cHolding Pools\u201d \u2014 offers users an easier way to passively earn a yield with their tokens, though with several innovations including a modular<\/a>, multi-pool<\/a> design enabling pools to adapt and transition strategies behind the scenes. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
