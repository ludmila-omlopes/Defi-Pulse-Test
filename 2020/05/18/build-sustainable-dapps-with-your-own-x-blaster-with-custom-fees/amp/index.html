TLDR: Integrating DEX.AG’s X Blaster with custom fees into your platform provides your users or smart contract with convenient native liquidity while allowing you to build a sustainable business model.

Liquidity is vital for the growth of DeFi

Users want to experience all DeFi has to offer. But often times to do that you need to swap between tokens or stablecoins. Liquidity is an important resource that enables DeFi’s composability and makes it possible for many DApps to function. It’s the blood that flows through the veins of DeFi.

For a smooth user experience, it’s essential to have a reliable liquidity at a decent price. DEXes are an obvious choice for reliable liquidity when designing an application or smart contract. However, relying on a single DEX comes with its disadvantages. For example, maybe the token your users want to trade isn’t listed or has extremely low liquidity causing costly price slippage.

DeFi is all about having options

If one DEX isn’t ideal, The obvious answer is to do what DeFi does best with money legos and combine the awesome powers of DEXes. That’s why we built our other service DEX.AG, a DEX aggregator. DEX.AG’s X Blaster let’s you trade multiple DEXes and even splits trades across the combination of DEXes that results in the best price. Each DEX has its strengths, the X Blaster combines those strengths and connects users with the DEX or DEXes that best suits their needs. Not to mention, the X Blaster’s API has a blazing fast response time that delivers available trades within a fraction of a second so users never wait.

Custom fees enable flexible and sustainable business models

We built DEX.AG because we understand the power of bringing the community together and the value that can create. We see our proxy contracts with custom fees as an opportunity for teams build sustainable models and grow their communities.

We know how crucial the right business model can be to finding the perfect product-market fit. You choose the custom fees on your X Blaster proxy contract. Developers know their users best and this gives them the flexibility to grow their community on their terms.
